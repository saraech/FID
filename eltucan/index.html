Rules

We want you to have fun, as that is our main priority! However, we also to ensure that we are providing quality service and creating a safe environment for your friends and family. We created this basic set of rules to help eliminate confusion, reduce the likeliness of problems, and avoid havoc. We hope you can comply with us in effort to keep everyone happy! For any questions about these set rules please feel free to reach out to us at (407)293-3218, extension 4.

  • Must be 21 to party
  • You must meet our formal dresscode. Those who do not dress to impress will be denied entry
  • The only bags allowed in are clear see through bags
  • All VIP table reservations must be made beforehand (24 hour notice to accomodate)
  • Smoking tobacco products are only allowed in the labeled smoking-zones, which are located upstairs
  • No standing and or trying to ride the 360 degree rotating DJ booth
