About Me





I am currently pursing my Bachelors of Arts (B.A) in digital media at the University of Central Florida.

My passion lies in web and graphic design. I find the relationship between design and web/app development to be a powerful one. In the future, I hope to use my work as a means of brand expression.

Born and raised in Miami, Florida and of Cuban descent, I am mostly inspired by culture and personal identity.

If I'm not designing or coding, you will be likely to find me on a beach or in a nearby coffee shop.
